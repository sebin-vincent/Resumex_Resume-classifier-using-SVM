Our Services

1

CREATE YOUR PROFILE

Resumex the online resume classification platform, which classifies the resumes into the corresponding job category in which it belongs. Connect with us by simply creating your profile here

2

GET THE RESUMES CLASSIFIED

Resumex provides the facility to upload a single resume document or a set of resumes as a zip file and the resumes should be of pdf format. In Resumex singly uploaded resumes are classified and provided with their corresponding categories instantly. And the zip files can be downloaded as a new zip file containing the resumes with their corresponding classified job category.

3

DOWNLOAD RESUME

The category of the uploaded single resume will be displayed forthwith. The set of uploaded resumes can be downloaded as a zip file, containing folders with categorized resume files
